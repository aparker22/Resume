My Story

I am a full stack software developer currently located in Atlanta, GA. I graduate in May 2018 from the DigitalCrafts' Immersive Software Coding Bootcamp.

I spent the first chapter of my professional career in Social Work, where I held multiple director level positions within both non-profit and large government organizations. During this time, the desire to help and inspire people was the primary driver behind my 10+ year experience. I began teaching myself to code after identifying areas that I felt could be improved through utilizing technology. I quickly discovered that I have a passion for coding and decided to pursue coding fulltime.

I am excited about my future in development. The more I learn, the more I love it. I enjoy the constant challenges, the daily (hourly!) learning, and the feeling of accomplishment when things work! Although no longer in the social work field, I remain passionate about working with both individuals and teams to pursue and meet goals.
